Free Consultation

Schedule a free, no-obligation consultation session with one of our business managers where they will:

  • Review the current state of your project
  • Ask questions about your needs and ultimate goals
  • Answer any questions you may have for us

We will gather all the information necessary to have our experts giv you an accurate and fair quote for the services you require.

Fair Price Quote

Many factors contribute to the cost of a smart contract audit including, but not limited to:

  • Project Complexity
  • Audit Depth
  • Number of Engineers & Hours
  • The Timeline

Analysis & Execution

Once our quote is accepted, our experts will begin their careful analysis of every aspect of your contract.

  • Thorough review of your code and documentation
  • Automated analyses on the code
  • Depending on audit depth, a manual line-by-line code review may be performed by multiple auditors

Once these analyses have been performed, the engineers will get together and discuss any differences in their findings. A consensus will be reached on solutions and recommendations.

Detailed Report

A detailed report will be created documenting and identifying any potential vulnerabilities or security risks.

Your report will include:

  • The engineering hours spent analysing your contract
  • An audit breakdown including specific tests ran and software used
  • A findings summary including all potential threats and their risk levels

Double Check

Once your project team has had a chance to implement the changes recommended in our report, we are happy to check everything a second time.

  • Review documentatio and process changes
  • Rerun automated test suites
  • Regenerate a detailed report

Certification & Promotion

We will furnish an Amoeba Audit sticker to place on your website and we will publish your audit results on our website if you wish.

A certification sticker will show your users that:

  • You and your team take security seriously
  • Your code has been checked and certified by a reputable security vendor
  • Their funds are protected within professionally audited protocols

Ad audit will detect and then eliminate vulnerabilities in a smart contract which attackers could use to cause serious damage. It also conveys a sense of security to potential partners and investors.

Our specialists look for known vulnerabilities, logic errors, and access control issues. The exploitation of these issues by a malicious actor could cause serious financial damage to your company and your investors.

Our auditors investigate all known attack vectors including reentrancy attacks, short address attacks, replay attacks, reordering attacks, and many others. We also look for logic errors which could cause unexpected behavior.

Dependinng on complexity, an audit takes between 2 to 15 days. The timeline can be adjusted according to our customer's needs. For large projects, the audit may take 3 to 4 weeks.
